NMIMS GAME DEVELOPMENT CLUB

We are the NMIMS Game Development Club, formerly known as the NIMS Game Design & Dev Club. Our club's purpose is to promote and educate students about the world of game design and development. It welcomes students interested in different aspects of development whether it be creating art, writing stories, composing music, programming, business and marketing, or simply playing games.
